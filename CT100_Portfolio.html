A Sea of Data (And Trash)

Within the sea of data, we are consistently inundated with information, that at times, are just counterproductive with what we intended to look for. The extra websites or articles that do not add anything to what you need just feel like trash. And it all harkens back to the visualization of the sea. The sea with its various ecosystems and beautiful wildlife is destroyed by mere pieces of “trash”, very similar to how the flow of data is dispersed on the internet as a whole.
